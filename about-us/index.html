Who we are

We are (in the process of becoming) a Registered Community Organization with the city of Philadelphia, serving the Wister neighborhood of East Gemantown.

Who can join

Anyone can come to the meetings (first Tuesday of the month), residents who live, work or own property in the area we serve can become members. Email info@bayntonhill.org for more information.
